Hi there! I am a researcher at Skild AI working on robotic foundation models. Previously, I received my M.S. in Machine Learning from Carnegie Mellon University, where I had the privilege of working with Prof. Ruslan Salakhutdinov, Prof. Deepak Pathak, and Murtaza Dalal. Prior to that, I completed my B.S. in Computer Science at Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Xiangyu Yue and Prof. Lin Shao.

I am broadly interested in topics like deep reinforcement learning and generative world modeling, with a particular focus on their applications in robotics. Feel free to reach out if you have shared research interests. I am always open to collaboration and discussions!
